5 Volleyball Skills You Must Have

  • Serving
    • Serving starts the rally and is the most important skill. The serve puts the ball in play once the referee blows the first whistle.

  • Passing
    • Passing is important because it is the first contact made on the ball when a serve is being received.

  • Setting
    • Setting, generally the second pass and an overhand contact of the ball that allows the hitter to spike it over.

  • Hitting
    • Hitting, also known as spiking, is when a player makes contact with the ball, quickly swinging heir arm down after they jump and hitting the ball in a downward motion.

  • Blocking
    • Blocking is a skill where the player defending the hitter deflects, or stops the ball from hitting the ground, keeping the ball in play.
